About Us

CyberHub’s award-winning hardware includes high-performance gaming peripherals and Blade gaming laptops. Cyber’s software platform, with over 125 million users, includes CyberHub Synapse (an Internet of Things platform), Cyber Chroma RGB (a proprietary RGB lighting technology system supporting thousands of devices and hundreds of games/apps), and Cyber Cortex (a game optimizer and launcher). CyberHub also offers payment services for gamers, youth, millennials and Generation Z. CyberHub Gold is one of the world’s largest game payment services, and Cyber Fintech provides fintech services in emerging markets.
